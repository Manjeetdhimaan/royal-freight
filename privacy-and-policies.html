ROYAL FREIGHT PRIVACY

Royal Freight’s mission is to make global trade frictionless by bringing logistics providers, importers, exporters and carriers online, making international freight easy and transparent. Similarly, it is important to us that we are transparent about the data we collect about Royal Freight users (aka you) and how we use that data. This privacy policy (the “Privacy Policy”) applies when you use Royal Freight Limited services, and any of its affiliated companies (the “Royal Freight”) products and websites. We offer our users choices about the data we collect, use and share as described in this Privacy Policy and our Help Center. We greatly respect your privacy, which is why we make every effort to provide services that would live up to the highest of user privacy standards. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully, so you can fully understand our practices in relation to personal data. “Personal data” or “personal information” means any information that can be used, alone or together with other data, to uniquely identify any living human being. When this Privacy Policy refers to “data“, this may include personal data, as well as business data which is not personal data. Please note that this is a master privacy policy and some of its provisions only apply to individuals in certain jurisdictions. If you have any questions, comments or inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the contact information below.

01. Introduction

Royal Freight operates a suite of web applications to support digital freight sales, booking, and management for logistics professionals. Our Privacy Policy applies to any website visitor (“Visitor“) or registered user (“User“) of Royal Freight. Our Users and Visitors use Royal Freight to compare freight shipping options, educate themselves with regards to freight processes or current market conditions, book and pay for their shipments, manage them, and coordinate and communicate with their respective logistics providers. This involves sharing information regarding shipment routes, cargo details, and business information about themselves and their logistics needs and services.

2. Services

This Privacy Policy, including our Cookie Policy, applies to your use of our Services. This Privacy Policy applies to this site, Royal Freight Market Intelligence services and other Royal Freight-related sites, apps, communications and services (“Services”), including off-site Services, such as our freight support staff, but excluding services that explicitly state that they are offered under a different privacy policy.

3. Data Controllers and Contracting Parties

Any of the Royal Freight entities is a controller of your personal data provided to, or collected by or for, or processed in connection with such Royal Freight entity’s Services. As a Royal Freight Visitor or User, the collection, use and sharing of your personal data is subject to this Privacy Policy and our Cookie Policy, as they may be updated from time to time. As for the use and processing of business data that does not contain personal information, see our Terms of Use, available here.

4. Change

This Privacy Policy applies to your use of our Services on or after the date in the header. Royal Freight (“we” or “us”) can modify this Privacy Policy (although we try not to do that often), and if we make material changes to it, we will provide notice through our Services, or by other means, to provide you the opportunity to review the changes before they become effective. If you object to any changes, you may close your account here. You acknowledge that your continued use of our Services after we publish or send a notice about our changes to this Privacy Policy means that the collection, use and sharing of your personal data is subject to the updated Privacy Policy.

5. Data We Collect

To create an account you need to provide data including your company name, email address, some information about your shipping habits such as frequency, mobile number, and a password. Upon booking a shipment, you also provide a payment method. In some cases, you may invite additional users to access your account; we retain this data as well. We will collect data about your location country and preferences such as language. We collect all this data to allow you to open an account and use our services. Our legal basis for this is the need for collecting and processing this data in order to fulfill our side of the contract with you, and perform our Services

7. How We Protect your Information

7.1. We take great care in making sure the data you give us and the data we collect from you is well protected. Therefore, we implemented adequate and appropriate security measures, both physical and digital. Please note, however, that we cannot guarantee that the information will not be compromised as a result of unauthorized penetration to our servers.

7.2. Data security and protection is dependent on both the security of our systems, and the security of the User’s systems and devices. Please make sure you use adequately strong passwords, and that you do not share them with anyone.

8. How We Share Information

8.1. We use service providers and third parties to help us provide our Services (e.g., maintenance, analysis, audit, payments, fraud detection, marketing and development).

They may have access to some of your personal data as reasonably necessary to perform these tasks on our behalf, and are contractually obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.
